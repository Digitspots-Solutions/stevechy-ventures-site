About Akasa Petroleum Logistics Limited

Akasa Petroleum Logistics Limited (also trading as Akasa Bunkering Services Co) is a leading Nigerian oil and gas company specializing in crude oil marketing and comprehensive bunkering services. Based in Alimosho, Lagos, we serve clients across Nigeria's energy sector with professionalism, reliability, and industry expertise.

Our founder, a seasoned crude oil marketer, established Akasa with a vision to bridge critical gaps in Nigeria's petroleum supply chain. We understand the complexities of the Nigerian energy market and deliver tailored solutions that meet the demanding requirements of our clients.

At Akasa, we combine deep industry knowledge with operational excellence to ensure uninterrupted petroleum supply, competitive pricing, and regulatory compliance. Whether you require crude oil procurement, marine fuel supply, or logistics coordination, our team delivers results you can depend on.
